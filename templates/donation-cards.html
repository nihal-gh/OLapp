Looking to Volunteer?

More and more these days, people are looking for ways to volunteer in India.
There is something about this country that appeals to those wanting to make a difference in the world.
There are countless ways that visitors and volunteers can help out at our orphanage. Occasionally, things go so well that volunteers return time and again… some even end up joining us on staff.

We prefer to individually work out a timetable for each visitor that fits in with our children’s home duties, school, and tuition timetables.

  • Helping out on the farm/workshop
