About US

Orphan Link is a comprehensive database system designed to manage all aspects of orphanage operations, including child records, donor information, staff details, and activities tracking. Our mission is to provide a secure and efficient platform to ensure the well-being of children.

Features

  • Detailed Child Records Management
  • Efficient Donor Tracking System
  • Comprehensive Staff Database
  • Activity Logging and Reporting
  • Secure and User-Friendly Interface
